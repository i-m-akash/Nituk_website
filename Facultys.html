DR. MAHEEP SINGH

Assistant Professor

Dr. Maheep Singh has obtained his Ph.D. degree from Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur in the year 2019. He is currently working as an Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Institute of Technology (NIT), Uttarakhand since July, 2013. His research area of interest includes Image Processing and Computer Vision, Salient Object Detection, Machine Learning, Secret sharing of Images. He has supervised 03 M.Tech thesis and at present he is supervising 07 Ph.D. students.

DR.KAMAL KUMAR

Assistant Professor(HOD)

Dr. Kamal Kumar, obtained his PhD in 2014 from Thapar University, Patiala, India. He has obtained his M. Tech. and B. Tech. from Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra, India, in 2007 and 2000 respectively. Presently he is working as Assistant Professor in National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand since July 2018. Presently he is working as Head of Department, Computer Science and Engineering from Mar 2022 onwards. He has supervised 2 PhDs and 29 PG (M. Tech.). Presently, 04 PhD Scholars and 01 PG Scholar is working under his supervision in National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand.

PROF. LALIT KUMAR AWASTHI

DIRECTOR

Prof. Lalit Kumar Awasthi, is presently Professor and Director at National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand since 9th February 2022. Earlier he served five-year term as Director of NIT Jalandhar and had additional charge of NIT Delhi (for about 4 months) and NIT Hamirpur from 14th August 2020 to 9th October 2021. Thereafter he was officiating Director at NIT Hamirpur till 3rd February 2022. Prof. Awasthi has an iconic track record of quality teaching, research and academic administration and institution building
