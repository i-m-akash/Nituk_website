ACADEMICS

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is an integral part of National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand. Department has 09 Faculty members including one trainee teacher for fostering quality education to students. All Faculty members are PhD from reputed Institutes. All the faculties are energetic, enthusiastic and help students in many respects such as counselling, inspiring young minds to grab better opportunities. Department offers UG program B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, PG program M. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering with two specializations namely, Artificial Intelligence and Computing Systems. Currently the department supports 36 seats in UG program and 15 seats in PG besides 05 seats in PG under self-sponsored scheme. Department offers PhD in relevant domain of Computer Science and Engineering such as cloud computing, natural language processing, pattern recognition, machine learning, network security, image processing, distributed systems etc.. A good number of relevant software are available for students and scholars in the department. Through its courses and curriculum sync with the present and future requirements, the department is an icon of NIT Uttarkhhhand. The Computer Science and Engineering Department works as a facilitating department for the Institute in terms of Internet Connectivity, Wi-Fi facility in the whole campus and surveillance of the entire Campus. The Department is equipped with the latest computers with UPS facilities for students, scholars and faculty. Department has bagged nearly 100 percent placements and internships for its eligible students in recent years. The Department provides enough opportunities for students to grow by organizing and participating in workshops, guest lectures, coding events, cultural and technical events held in the Institute besides the academic activities.

PLACEMENT

NIT Uttarakhand was established in 2009. Since its inception, this institute has grown in many fields and has established itself as one of the best Technical Education Institutes. Our graduates excel at the workplace and in society in general as our curriculum emphasizes technical knowledge, communication, teamwork and life-long learning skills. To broaden the knowledge and to receive industrial exposure, our students from undergraduate engineering streams undergo practical training during summer vacation and six-month internship in reputed industries/institutions/organizations across India and abroad as well. Our postgraduate students also visit reputed industries/institutions/organizations for their project and research works.
